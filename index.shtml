Overview

The University of Texas at Austin provides a unique opportunity for graduate and post-graduate training in computational and theoretical neuroscience. The Computational and Theoretical Neuroscience Group comprises faculty focusing on computational modeling, experiments, and theory.

Our philosophy is that progress in understanding neural computation depends on a tight link between theory and quantitative experimental measurements. Members of the the Computational and Theoretical Neuroscience Group are immersed in a vibrant interdisciplinary environment. Experiment-theory collaborations here span the range from molecular to circuit to cognitive neuroscience.
